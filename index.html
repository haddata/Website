What is Cloud Cabinet?

A fast, reliable and simple way to store your data

Have you ever had messed up folders and files, that you could not manage to keep tidy? If so, CloudCabinet is the best solution! It applies an inovative, yet simple and intuitive way to store your data.

Why would you chose CloudCabinet?

Reliable

CloudCabinet uses Amazon S3 as the base engine to keep your files. That way, your data is backed up and protected.

Simple and Smart

CloudCabinet uses the oldfashioned but yet inovative way to store files. You will never have to worry about tidiness again, because CloudCabinet does that for you!

24/7 Support

CloudCabinet administrators are always there to answer any questions you have, or solve any problems

Responsive on all devices

Cloud Cabinet is highly responsive and it works on all devices! Have all the files with you whereever you go!

How it works?

Very similar to the real-life file storage! You have an online Cabinet, hosted on our cloud. Inside the cabinet, as you may probably think, there are Drawers! Every Drawer has folders and this is where you save your files - in folders! That way, You will save your files where you should - in proper folders and it will be tidy all the time!

How to start?

All you have to do is join CloudCabinet by creating an account. Once your account is created, you get your Cabinet ready, for free!
